About Us

The Official Technology club of Techno India Hooghly -Cybertronix, was established by Soumyadeep De, Sankhajeet Taran, Ranit Ghosh, Debdutta Das, Uddipan Dey & Tamal Dolui to inspire and promote thinkings that are beyond classrooms. At present there are approximately 100-150 members in it.

Commencement

The official Technology Club of Techno India Hooghly -Cybertronix, was established in August 2018. It was established to inspire young minds and to enrich the technical culture inside the College Campus and grow together. Cybertronix has been paving the way for bright and zealous enthusiasts of technology, helping them innovate and create all the while learning important management skills.

Ideation

With the spirit, to help in growing the futuristic ideas of young minds the club Cybertronix is established and that's led to Spark Quest.The first-ever independent technological fest of our college in 2018. Support flourished through the young minds and Faculty Members making it possible for a first grand Virtual Event. The popularity led to the foundation of our very first website for https://sparkquest.in

CodeChef TCH Chapter

Codechef TCH Chapter is a competitive programming community under Codechef Organisation. The club is formed under Techno India Hooghly College and was started in November 2020. The club is maintained by the official chapter leaders under codechef supervision. Codechef TCH chapter aims at promoting competitive programming among peers by improving their basics, problem solving-skills through various activities like contests, quizzes etc.

Spark Quest 2.O

Born in 2018, with the soulful will of promoting technology, scientific thinking & innovation. Spark Quest has surpassed 11 years of creating an unmatched aura of a science & technology spectacle every year, at the campus of Techno India Hooghly at Chinsurah, West Bengal. In its aspiration to provide a platform to the student to portray their skill sets and talents in all kind of events. Spark Quest strives for one and all to get inspired & look up to.
